About MS Tour and Travel

Your trusted partner in creating unforgettable travel experiences for over 25 years

Our Story

Founded in 2000, MS Tour and Travel Visa Services began as a small travel agency with a big vision: to make international travel accessible and hassle-free for everyone. What started as a modest operation with just three employees has grown into one of the most trusted travel companies in India.

Our journey began when our founder, Mr. Manoj Kumar Singh, recognized the challenges people faced when planning international trips - complex visa processes, confusing travel arrangements, and the stress of coordinating multiple services. He envisioned a one-stop solution that would handle all travel needs with expertise and care.

Today, we're proud to have helped over 10,000+ travelers explore the world, with a team of 40+ dedicated professionals and offices in three major Indian cities. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction remains the cornerstone of our success.
